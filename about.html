About Us

Welcome to Pyro-Techz, where the celebration never stops! For over a decade, we have been providing communities with the safest, most spectacular consumer fireworks on the market. From towering aerial repeaters to family-friendly sparklers, our inventory is hand-selected to ensure your events are unforgettable.

What sets us apart is the PyroTV system. With it, you can see exactly what you are buying before you buy it! No more guessing based off of packaging. Just scan the barcode with the scanner, and the video will play for you.

We believe every holiday, wedding, and celebration deserves a little extra spark. Contact us for special events where you need some boom!
